Welcome

Welcome to the official site of Adrian Mauro—an actor, stunt performer and producer born in Houston, Texas. Best known for his role as Roberto in FX’s acclaimed series Atlanta, Adrian has built a career at the intersection of performance and physical storytelling. More than simply an actor, his athletic background led him to the world of stunts, allowing him to bring dynamic action to screen and to work behind the camera as a second‑unit and assistant director [1]. He has continued to expand his repertoire with roles in a range of independent features and shorts—from the horror‑comedy Gothic Slayers and the drama Choices to the offbeat Casting and genre‑bending projects like 7, Mirage and Make It Rain—demonstrating a versatile on‑screen presence.
