Biography

Adrian Mauro is an actor, stunt performer, and producer originally from Houston, Texas, now active in Atlanta’s thriving film scene. Known for his athletic background and martial arts training in Kuk Sool Won and boxing, Mauro transitioned from sports into film performance and stunt work. He has built a multifaceted career, combining on-screen acting, behind-the-scenes production, and stunt coordination. In 2022, Mauro guest-starred on FX’s acclaimed series Atlanta, portraying Roberto, an aspiring rapper, in the fan-favorite “Crank Dat Killer” episode.
