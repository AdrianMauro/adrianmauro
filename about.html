About Adrian

Biography

Adrian Mauro was born and raised in Houston, Texas. With an innate passion for performance, he grew from community theatre into independent film sets before landing his breakout role as Roberto in FX’s award‑winning series Atlanta[1]. His athletic upbringing fostered a love of sports that naturally led him into the world of stunts[2]; from fight choreography to high falls, he brings a fearless physicality to his characters. This versatility extended into stunt performance on other productions, including a credit as a stunt performer on the drama series Mayans M.C. in 2022[5]. In addition to his high‑profile work on Atlanta, Adrian has embraced a diverse slate of independent features and shorts. He has embodied characters in the horror‑comedy Gothic Slayers, the drama Choices, the offbeat short Casting, and the thrillers 7, Mirage and Make It Rain, reflecting his ability to move fluidly across genres and formats.

Beyond acting, Adrian has found a creative home on the other side of the camera. He has served as a second‑unit and assistant director on science‑fiction TV series such as More Than Human[3] and the upcoming film Gothic Slayers[3]. His experiences in production deepened his appreciation for the business and logistics of filmmaking[2], ultimately inspiring him to co‑produce projects like the short thriller 7 and the TV mini‑series Mirage[4].

Outside of set life, Adrian trains in martial arts, boxing and parkour to maintain the agility required for stunts. He is dedicated to continuous growth—both artistically and physically—and to helping craft stories that resonate long after the credits roll.
